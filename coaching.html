Welcome to the Future of Coaching Education

SCC Coach Training is now SCC Coaching Education, reimagining the future of global accreditation and leading the way with an innovative model to enhance the standard of excellence when it comes to coaching education. Visit the Evolution of the SCC Accreditation page to learn more.

Introducing ICF Imagine: The Global Conference on Coaching Education and Trainings

Credibility for Your Coaching Organization

SCC Coaching Education provides accredited providers with a competitive edge both in their content and stature. Coaches worldwide recognize SCC as an industry leader and turn to us for information about initial and continuing education. SCC accreditation will give your program unquestioned credibility and the ability to expose your services to a wider network of coaches.
