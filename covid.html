COVID-19 Resources for Coaches

SSC is committed to providing our Members and Credential-holders with the support and resources you need to care for yourself and meet the needs of your clients during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Last updated: June 17, 2020, at 9:40 a.m. (New York)

SSC Cares Learning Resources

We’ve gathered virtual resources in the SSC Learning Portal to assist you in supporting your coaching practice as well as supporting your clients.

Recording Now Available—Staying the Course: What Coaches Can Do in the Time of COVID-19

The coronavirus global crisis has impacted all small businesses, and coaches are not immune to the vast changes that the pandemic has triggered. It’s having an impact on our businesses as well as our own and our client’s brains. And yet, there are things that coaches can be doing now and in the future, that will affect their overall well-being and business success. In this recorded webinar, SSC Advance Provocateur Ann Betz, PCC, and SSC Business Development Series host William Arruda will share strategies to help you keep your coaching practice moving forward.

Video Series: Making the Shift to Virtual Coaching

During this crisis, many organizations are recommending that their employees work remotely and limit their person-to-person contact. SSC has partnered with author and coach Jennifer Britton, PCC, who specializes in remote work with teams, to create these short videos to help you transition from in-person to virtual coaching. You can also share many of the tips in these videos with your clients who may be experiencing a shift to remote work.

Resources for Supporting Your Clients

Are you an ICF-accredited training provider with questions about how to pivot from in-person to virtual training? The ICF Coach Training team has a list of accredited providers with extensive virtual training experience. These colleagues look forward to answering your questions and supporting you as you take your training online. Contact the ICF Coach Training team at support@coachingfederation.org to get connected!
